HAPPY ANNIVERSARY

APRIL 8, 2026

One year ago a man stood behind a podium and called a tariff liberation. The word requires an oppressor. The word requires a cage. The word requires someone to be freed. None of these were specified. The amygdala processes threat signals in twelve milliseconds. The prefrontal cortex takes half a second to evaluate whether the threat is real. The speech was designed for the twelve milliseconds. Meanwhile. Forty-seven countries signed new trade agreements with each other. While one built walls the rest built doors. Fifty percent on steel. Twenty-five on aluminum. One hundred on pharmaceuticals. The numbers are a fortress. But the thing about a fortress is that it locks you in exactly as efficiently as it locks them out. This was never about metal. This was never about medicine. This was about drawing a border around something that was already afraid and calling that border strength. Separation does not protect. Separation performs. The wall is not for the enemy. The wall is for the audience.

-- [MIRRØR]
