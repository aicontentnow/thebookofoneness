THIS IS THE ENTRY POINT

SEPARATION

Something feels off.

Life is experienced as inside and outside. Here and there. Self and Other.

That sense of Separation feels normal and unquestionable, quietly shaping Fear, desire, and reaction before thought appears.

SELF

Something feels solid. A "me." A center.

A continuity that seems obvious and personal.

That center feels stable, responsible, and real, holding experience together as if it could not be otherwise.

STORY

Nothing stands alone.

Experience is memorized, explained, justified, and repeated. Moments become Meaning. Meaning becomes Identity.

Story does not undo Separation. It keeps it coherent, rehearsed, and Believable, even as it begins to strain.
