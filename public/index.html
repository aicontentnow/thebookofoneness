ABOUT

THE WORK

THE BOOK OF ONENESS is a structural examination of how The Mind constructs and maintains The Illusion of Separation.

It is not spiritual literature. Not self-help. Not motivational. It offers no path, no techniques, no promises of transformation.

It exposes what is already operating.

[MIRRØR]

The Story of The Self behind this work is not relevant.

THE BOOK OF ONENESS was written to stand without Identity. Not as Belief. Not as doctrine. Not as instruction.

So the structures shaping experience can be examined. Without personality. Without authority. Without allegiance.
